# Business Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes business sales data to uncover revenue trends, product performance, category profitability, and regional sales patterns. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to transform raw data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Business Problem

Organizations generate large volumes of sales data but often struggle to convert it into meaningful insights. This project aims to answer key business questions:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* How do sales change over time?
* Which categories are the most profitable?
* Which regions contribute the most to business growth?
* What actions can improve overall performance?

## Dataset

The analysis was performed using a retail sales dataset containing:

* Order and shipping information
* Product and category details
* Customer information
* Sales, profit, and discount metrics
* Regional and state-level data

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Microsoft Excel

## Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed through:

* Removing duplicates
* Handling missing values
* Correcting data types
* Formatting date fields
* Creating calculated columns and measures
* Building a structured data model

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Profit Margin (%)

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Performance Analysis

Tracks sales growth and revenue trends over time.

### Product Performance

Identifies top-selling and highest-revenue products.

### Category Analysis

Evaluates category and sub-category contribution to revenue and profit.

### Regional Analysis

Compares sales performance across different regions and states.

### Customer Insights

Highlights high-value customers and purchasing behavior.

### Profitability Analysis

Analyzes the relationship between sales and profit.

## Key Insights

* Sales demonstrated consistent growth with strong seasonal peaks.
* A small number of products contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Technology emerged as the highest-performing category.
* Regional performance varied considerably, indicating expansion opportunities.
* Certain products generated high sales but lower profitability due to discounts.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase focus on high-performing products and categories.
2. Optimize discount strategies to improve profit margins.
3. Invest marketing efforts in high-growth regions.
4. Develop targeted campaigns for underperforming markets.
5. Implement customer retention programs for top-value customers.
